# Privacy Policy for InviteManager Last Updated: June 2026 ## Introduction InviteManager ("the Bot", "we", "our", or "us") is a Discord bot designed to provide invite tracking, server growth analytics, leaderboards, and server management features. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. ## Information We Collect To provide its services, InviteManager may collect and store the following information: * Discord User IDs * Discord Server (Guild) IDs * Discord Channel IDs * Discord Role IDs * Invite codes and invite statistics * Member join and leave records * Server configuration settings * Command usage data necessary for bot functionality InviteManager does not collect or store: * Discord passwords * Email addresses * Payment information * Private messages * Message content beyond what is required to process commands ## How We Use Information The information collected is used solely for: * Tracking server invites * Generating invite leaderboards * Providing server growth statistics * Managing invite reward systems * Delivering bot commands and requested features * Maintaining server-specific configuration ## Data Storage Data is stored securely on systems controlled by InviteManager and is retained only as long as necessary to provide the bot's functionality. ## Sharing of Information InviteManager does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties except when required by law or to comply with legal obligations. ## Message Content InviteManager may temporarily process message content to execute prefix commands. Message content is not stored, logged, used for advertising, or used to train machine learning or artificial intelligence models. ## User Rights Server owners may request removal of their server's stored data by contacting the bot administrator. Users may request information regarding stored data associated with their Discord account. ## Security We take reasonable measures to protect stored information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. ## Contact If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the InviteManager support team through the official Discord support server.
